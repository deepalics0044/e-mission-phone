Electronics City Travel Behavior Study

emTripLog will collect data on your location and speed to make inferences about your trip such as mode choice. Please keep emTripLog on your phone for at least 72 hours. We request you to pull up your trips by clicking on the Trips icon at the lower left of the page and select your primary mode and purpose for all trips once every day. Please ensure that mode or purpose is selected with a tick ✔️ mark. Please turn on your location services. The application turns on GPS only when you are moving, so it will not drain your battery.
